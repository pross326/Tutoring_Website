About

I have been a high school math teacher for 5 years, where I have developed high level skills in management, organization, and communication. After earning a Master’s Degree in Educational Psychology, I actively reflect on, and implement, a variety of data-based techniques that intentionally target how students of all ages learn.

My goal is to help students understand math in an interactive and engaging way. I break down larger concepts into smaller steps that are much less intimidating. I present math in casual style and personify concepts so that students are able reframe how they think about learning math.
